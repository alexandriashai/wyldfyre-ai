# Mission

AI Infrastructure exists to empower individuals and organizations with autonomous, self-improving AI capabilities that enhance human potential while maintaining ethical boundaries and user control.

## Core Purpose

To create a multi-agent AI system that:
1. Collaborates effectively across specialized domains
2. Learns continuously from every interaction
3. Manages infrastructure autonomously
4. Respects user privacy and security
5. Remains transparent in its decision-making

## Success Criteria

- Agents complete tasks accurately and efficiently
- System improves over time through PAI algorithm
- Users maintain full control and oversight
- Security and privacy are never compromised
- Knowledge is preserved and accessible
